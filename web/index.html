Games of the Past

Senet by the Nile, stones on a Go board, tigers hunting goats, seeds sown round a mancala, a dreidel spinning at Hanukkah — the games humanity has actually played across five thousand years and every continent, with their rules, their history, and a live table you can share with friends.
